Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we handle data in the Insighta AI Assistant research tool.

1. Data Collection & Processing

The Insighta AI Assistant is designed with privacy as a core principle. We implement the following practices:

  • No Personal Data Storage: Images uploaded or captured via camera are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Anonymous Processing: All analysis occurs without linking images to personal identifiers.
  • Transient Processing: Images are held in memory only for the duration of analysis (typically less than 2 seconds).
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor individual users.

2. Technical Implementation

  • Image processing occurs entirely in the user's browser session for camera analysis
  • Uploaded images are transmitted securely (HTTPS) for server-side analysis but are immediately deleted after processing
  • No personal health information (PHI) is collected or stored
  • Server logs are anonymized and rotated regularly

3. Research Data

For research improvement purposes, we may collect anonymized, aggregated statistics such as:

  • Total number of analyses performed
  • General performance metrics (accuracy, processing time)
  • Browser and device types (for compatibility improvement)

No individual images or analysis results are included in research datasets without explicit, informed consent through our formal research collaboration program.

Medical Disclaimer

Important Notice

The Insighta AI Assistant is a research tool for preliminary analysis only. It is not a medical device and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

1. Limitations of AI Analysis

  • AI models have inherent limitations and can make errors
  • Performance varies across different skin types, conditions, and image quality
  • The tool does not consider patient history, symptoms, or physical examination
  • Confidence scores indicate model certainty, not clinical certainty

2. When to Seek Professional Care

Always consult a qualified dermatologist or healthcare provider if you have concerns about:

  • New, changing, or growing skin lesions
  • Lesions that bleed, itch, or cause discomfort
  • Family history of skin cancer
  • Any concerning skin changes

In emergency situations, seek immediate medical attention.

3. No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of this tool does not create a doctor-patient relationship. The research team are not providing medical advice through this platform.

Ethical Guidelines

Our commitment to ethical AI development and responsible research practices.

1. Research Ethics Compliance

  • All research follows institutional review board (IRB) protocols
  • Data collection for research improvement requires informed consent
  • We prioritize beneficence and non-maleficence in tool design
  • Regular ethical reviews of algorithms and applications

2. Algorithmic Fairness

We actively work to address potential biases in our AI systems:

  • Diverse training datasets representing multiple skin types
  • Regular bias audits and performance evaluations across subgroups
  • Transparency about known limitations and performance variations
  • Ongoing efforts to improve equity in diagnostic accuracy

3. Transparency & Accountability

  • Clear communication about tool capabilities and limitations
  • Provision of confidence scores to indicate uncertainty
  • Openness about research methodology and validation results
  • Responsible disclosure of errors or limitations discovered
